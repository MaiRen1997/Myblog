## must VS  have to

must : 是自己自发做的

have to: 是基于客观因素不得不做

I have to follow the law

I have to drive slowly on this road

## long VS tall

long 表示横向长

tall表示竖向

The building is tall

the road is long

## at VS on VS in 表示时间

1. year、seasons、month用in
2. day用on
3. hour用at

::: tip 

具体的日期，星期几，年月日，都用on

on December 25th 2024

on Monday

:::

## 早中晚搭配

5. at  dusk(4:00~6:00)
6. in the evening(5:00~10:00a)
