Riverside Joy
2024-06-12

20240606

You all had some tea?你们都喝茶了吗？

OK. Biscuits, perhaps? 好的。 也许是饼干？

OK. So we all love biscuits, right?好的。 所以我们都喜欢饼干，对吧？

Audience: Yes. 观众：是的。

So join me now on a story and a journey of India’s -- one of the most popular brands of biscuits. 现在就和我一起了解 "印度 最受欢迎的饼干品牌之一的故事和旅程吧。

Let's meet Devraj Pai, a small farmer who grows wheat on his one-hectare farm in arid Gujarat.让我们来认识一下 Devraj Pai，他是 "一位小农， 在干旱的古吉拉特邦拥有一公顷的农场种植小麦"

This wheat is harvested and trucked 42 kilometers to a government marketplace, and there it is bought by a trader, who then sells it on 100 kilometers further to a commercial flour mill.这些小麦被收割后用 卡车运至 42 公里外的" 政府市场，在 那里由贸易商购买， "然后再将其出售给 100 公里外的" 一家商业面粉厂

At the mill, it is stripped of all of its nutrients and fiber and then converted to refined flour.在工厂，它被剥夺了 所有的营养物质和纤维，" 然后转化为精制面粉

And then it is trucked another 400 kilometers further to a larger factory to be mixed with refined sugar, which has had a similar long journey.然后再用卡车运往 400 公里外的" "一家更大的工厂，与 精制糖混合，精制糖也" 经历了类似的长途运输

All of this is mixed with chemical preservatives, artificial flavors, and then baked into biscuits using energy-guzzling machines and then trucked 500 kilometers back to the same villages.所有这些都与化学 防腐剂、人工香料混合，" "然后使用耗能机器烘烤成饼干， " 然后用卡车运回 500 公里外的同 一个村庄

Now consider this.现在考虑一下这一点

Devraj Pai sold his wheat for 22 rupees a kilo, and then he buys this stripped-down, sugary unhealthy biscuits for his family at 120 rupees a kilo, almost five times more.Devraj Pai 以每公斤 22 卢比的价格出售了他的小麦 ，" 然后他以每公斤 120 卢比的价格为家人购买了这种精简的 含糖不健康饼干 ， 几乎是后者的五倍

This is just the tip of the math iceberg.这只是数学冰山一角

Underneath lies the hidden world of margins at multiple points, fuel costs, infrastructure costs and another painful, unaccounted, hidden cost.其 "背后隐藏着 多个点的利润、" 燃料成本、 基础设施成本 "以及另一个令人痛苦的、 无法解释的隐性成本

The cost to nature.自然付出的代价

All this in the guise of modern efficiency and scale?这一切都是打着现代 效率和规模的幌子吗？

What the f-- truck are we not seeing here?我们在这里没看到什么该死的卡车？

(Laughter and applause)（笑声和掌声）

This is a tiny example of the highly polluting industrial farming model that is eating the world.这是正在吞噬世界的 "高污染 工业化农业模式的一个小例子

Let's zoom out and view this. No escaping it.让我们缩小并查看这一点。无法逃避它

India is the world's most populous country, yet it is still pretty low, among the lowest in terms of per capita greenhouse gas emissions.印度是世界上 人口最多的国家，" 但其 "人均 温室气体排放量仍然很低，属于最低国家之一。

Now, for an India under construction, rapidly modernizing, this is the fifth largest economy, shortly to be the fourth.现在，对于一个正在建设中、 迅速现代化的印度来说，" 它是第五大经济体， 很快就会成为第四大经济体

For Indians on consumption steroids, it is third in terms of purchasing power parity.对于服用类固醇消费的印度人来说， " 按购买力平价计算，印度排名第三

A story of growth that is inexorably interlinked with global warming.这是一个与全球变暖密不可分的增长故事。

How green is our current or future?我们的当前或未来有多环保

As brown as my face, friends.和 我的脸一样棕色，朋友们

Contributing 20 percent to this browning of the planet is agriculture, with over half the country's workforce engaged in it. 农业 "对地球的褐变贡献了20% ，" "全国一半以上的 劳动力从事农业

Staring us in the face is this absolute imperative to power green production and empower green consumption.摆在我们面前的 "是 推动绿色生产" 、赋能绿色消费的绝对必要性

A daunting task for India's 150 million small farmers who own less than two hectares of land.对于印度 " 拥有不到两公顷土地的 1.5 亿小农来说，这是一项艰巨的任务

Now I grew up in awe of nature as I tagged along with my civil engineer father in the deepest jungles of India.现在， "当我 跟随我的土木工程师父亲" 在印度最深处的丛林中成长时，我对大自然充满了敬畏

My mother, she taught me respect for Indigenous foods, cultures and the kinship of communities.我的母亲，她教会我 尊重土著食物、文化" 和社区的亲缘关系

A 25-year digression into the modern food industry.涉足现代食品工业 25 年

Traveling the world was a roller coaster ride of the good, the bad, and the ugly of what the farmer grows to what lands up on a consumer's plate.环游世界就像坐过山车一样，从农民种的东西到消费者盘子里的东西，有 好的、坏的" 和丑陋的

So my midlife crisis coincided with the climate crisis, and that motivated me to set up India Foundation for Humanistic Development with a vision to rejuvenate productive landscapes equitably and sustainably.因此，我的中年危机 与气候危机同时发生，" 这促使我成立了 "印度 人文发展基金会，" "其愿景是 " 公平、可持续地振兴生产力景观

We work with small farmers across India, as well as Indigenous communities in 22 states to promote natural farming, green enterprise development and habitat conservation.我们与印度各地的小农 "以及 22 个邦的原住民社区合作，" 促进自然农业、 "绿色企业发展 和栖息地保护

I see my work as knitting, knitting a patchwork quilt of stories, of communities, heir challenges, and the change makers and, you know, presenting this vibrant, multi-hued, expanding and evolving saga to the powers that be.我认为我的工作就是编织， "编织一个由 故事、社区、" 他们的挑战 和变革者拼凑而成的被子， "并且，你知道，向当权者呈现 这个充满活力、多彩、不断" "扩展和发展的传奇故事

And ... 而且……

I would like to now take you to a golden patch, as I zoom in, on a golden patch in Belgaum district of Karnataka in the south, where the heroes of my story are 10,000 small farmers.我现在想带你们去看看南部卡纳塔克邦 "贝尔高姆区的一片黄金地 ，" "我的故事里的英雄 是那里的 10,000 个小农

This story brings to life how green production can lead to green consumption in a more equitable and sustainable way.这个故事生动地讲述了绿色生产如何 " 以更公平和可持续的方式带来绿色消费

Let's meet Anand.让我们来认识一下阿南德

He's a small farmer growing maize in Belgaum on a two-hectare farm.他是 贝尔高姆的一位小农，在占地两公顷的农场里种植玉米

Anand is also, you know, the proud managing director of the Saudati Farmer Producer Company Limited.您知道，阿南德也是 " "沙特农民 生产者有限公司令人自豪的董事总经理

Saudati Company has 1,000 farmers like our Anand, who are shareholders and co-owners.Saudati 公司有 1000 名 像我们 Anand 这样的农民，" 他们是股东和共同所有者

Their collective strength allows them to operate at scale, to raise finance, to bulk purchase farm inputs, to eliminate middlemen and then to trade directly with big buyers for higher prices.他们的集体力量 使他们能够大规模经营，" 筹集资金， "批量购买农业投入品， 消除中间商，" "然后直接 与大买家以更高的价格进行交易

By improving their farming efficiency, the Saudati farmers are earning at least 25 percent more and break away from high-interest micro loans that they needed before.通过提高农业效率， "沙特农民的收入 至少增加了 25%，" "并摆脱了 以前所需的高息小额贷款

They also get better-than-market prices for their produce from Anand and his team during season.在季节期间，他们还从阿南德和他的团队那里获得优于市场的产品价格

Now, the profits from Saudati company are invested back into the company or distributed to the farmer shareholders at the end of the year, and this is a decision that is jointly taken.现在，Saudati公司的利润 是在年底重新投资到公司 "还是分配给农民股东 ，" "这是 共同做出的决定。"

How green is my valley?我的山谷有多绿？

To know this, let's visit the 1,000 co-owners of Saudati company, who are transitioning from a chemical-based monocrop agriculture to an organic, multi-crop farming system at scale through our natural farming trainings.要了解这一点， "让我们走访 Saudati 公司的 1,000 名共同所有者 ，" 他们正在通过我们的自然农业培训，从 基于化学的单一作物农业大规模过渡到有机 "多作物 农业系统

We track and trace their production practices through an app called FoodSign, which allows us to capture the tons of carbon that is sequestered on their lands.我们通过一款名为 FoodSign 的应用程序跟踪和追踪 他们的生产实践" ， "该应用程序使我们能够捕获 " 他们土地上封存的大量碳

This is then traded to earn carbon revenues to create assets that would benefit the whole community.然后将其 "进行交易 以赚取碳收入，" "以创造造福 整个社区的资产 This could be a production facility, this could be a common warehouse or a primary health center, or even a drinking-water facility.这可以是生产设施， "可以是公共仓库 或初级保健中心，" 甚至是饮用水设施

Their decision.他们的决定

Now it does pay to green production, right?现在它确实有利于绿色 生产，对吗？

Let's now meet the heroines of my story who power the green production to green consumption pipeline.现在让我们来认识一下我故事中的女主角，她们为 "绿色生产 和绿色消费管道提供动力

Meet Kamala and her 12-member self-help group, who are shareholders of Saudati.来认识一下 Kamala 和她的 12 名 自助小组，他们是 Saudati 的股东

And they run a micro enterprise and yeah, they produce biscuits. 他们经营一家微型企业， 是的，他们生产饼干

Whole wheat and millet flour is mixed with jaggery.全麦和小米面粉 与粗糖混合

A jaggery is an iron-rich brown sugar.粗糖是一种富含铁的红糖

This is baked in ovens that run on solar or biomass energy.这是在使用 太阳能或生物质能的烤箱中烘烤的

Simply packed and sold locally.简单包装并在当地销售

Our Doorstep incubator program supports nine such farmer-producer companies, with 9,000 shareholders in Belgaum district who are linked to 150 micro enterprises, which are run by women and youth who just add value to local produce for local markets using renewable energy.我们的“上门孵化器”计划 "支持 9 家此类 农民生产商公司，" " 贝尔高姆地区有 9,000 名股东，" 他们与 150 家微型企业有联系， 这些企业由妇女和年轻人经营， 他们只是 利用可再生能源为当地市场增加当地产品的价值

Let's step back and think a bit.让我们退后一步 思考一下

Do you see the simple, fantastic math happening here?你看到这里发生的简单而奇妙的 数学了吗？

A lone, two-hectare subsistence farmer transforms into this joint-venture of a 10,000 farmers managing 20,000 hectares of produce and earning multi-million rupees.一个孤独的、两公顷的自给农民 转变为这家由 10,000 名农民组成的合资企业， 管理 20,000 公顷的农产品 并赚取数百万卢比

A local circular food economy.地方循环食品经济

That has the impact to ... benefit a whole district with a million population.这会产生影响......使 "整个地区的 百万人口受益

Mind boggling, but doable.令人难以置信，但可行

This scenario is something that can be repeated or replicated 100,000 times across India.这种场景可以 " 在印度各地重复或重复10万次

And ... Secure the right to ... affordable, sufficient and culturally appropriate food for a billion Indians.并且…… 确保十亿印度人获得……负担 得起的、充足的 "和文化上适宜的食物的权利

This is a reset button that connects culture to policy, and to reverse and repair a broken food system to a green and fair food system.这是一个重置按钮，将 文化与政策联系起来，并将 破碎的粮食系统扭转和修复为绿色和公平的粮食系统。

There are three things that underpin all that we do to let a thousand Belgaums bloom.为了让一千个贝尔高姆绽放，我们所做的一切都基于三件事

One is a farming system that is climate-resilient, uses existing natural resources to improve productivity and farm incomes, just as our 10,000 farmers are showing us.一是 具有气候适应能力的农业系统，利用现有自然资源 来提高生产力" 和农业收入， 正如我们的 10,000 名农民向我们展示的那样

Two, a production system that adds value close to source and conserves natural resources as well as allocates fair value to producer as well as consumer.第二，一种 在源头增加价值" 、 保护自然资源 "以及 为生产者" 和消费者分配公平价值的生产体系

We do know, I think by now, that Camilla's biscuits are surely tastier and healthier for our kids, right?我想现在我们确实知道卡米拉的饼干 " 对我们的孩子来说肯定更美味、更健康，对吧？

Three.三

A value system that protects nature and people's rights.保护自然 和人民权利的价值体系

The ten farmer-producer companies with their communities in Belgaum are a living example of equitable ownership and joint decision making. 贝尔高姆的 "十家农民生产公司 及其社区" "是公平 所有权和联合决策的活生生的例子

In Sanskrit, India's oldest language, we say (speaking Sanskrit).在印度最古老的语言梵语中， 我们说（说梵语）

This means “the world is one family.”这意味着“世界是一个家庭

Showing up where and when it matters, sharing and caring is my family trait.在 重要的时间和地点出现、 分享和关怀是我的家庭特质

For me, this means the world of food is in our collective hands.对我来说，这意味着食物的世界 掌握在我们的集体手中

And we must, we should learn to value food from the lens of climate and communities.我们必须、 我们应该学会 从气候和社区的角度来评价食物

You can do this.你可以这样做

By connecting to the journey of food.通过连接食物的旅程。

And choosing what lands up in your shopping cart.并选择 您购物车中的商品

Will you?你会
