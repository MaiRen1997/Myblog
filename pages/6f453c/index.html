不同表达

  1. I don't know
  2. I have no idea

  1. I made a mistake
  2. I screwed up

# 你在说什么

  1. what did you say
  2. come again

# 我们意见一致

  1. we are on the same page
  2. we see eye to eye with each other.

so therefore
I think in my opinion
and in addition
maybe perhaps/ likely, probably

  2. freeze

# 渴

  1. thirsty
  2. parched

# 大的

  1. big
  2. massive
